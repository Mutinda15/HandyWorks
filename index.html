Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ'S

How can I request a service through Handy Works?

Simply download the Handy Works app, create an account, and choose the service you need. Our professionals will be notified and will contact you to schedule the service at your convenience.

Are the service providers reliable and experienced?

Yes, all our service providers undergo a rigorous vetting process to ensure they have the skills and experience to provide top-notch service. You can trust Handy for all your needs!

How can I pay for services?

We offer various payment methods, including credit/debit cards and mobile money, to make your transactions smooth and secure. You can pay after the service is completed.

Can I track the status of my service request?

Yes! Once your service request is accepted, you can track the progress in real-time through the Handy Works app. Our team will keep you updated every step of the way.

What happens if I am not satisfied with the service?

If you're not completely satisfied with the service, let us know! We offer a satisfaction guarantee and will work with you to resolve any issues as quickly as possible.
